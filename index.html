Who We Are

A voice driven by disabled people, for disabled people.

CCD is a human rights organization that has advocated for the full and equal participation of Canadians with disabilities since 1976. Our membership comprises national, provincial, and territorial organizations of persons with disabilities.

We believe that people with disabilities are the foremost experts on their own lives. All of our policy work, submissions, and advocacy positions are shaped by the people we represent.

From the courtroom to Parliament Hill, from international human rights bodies to local communities — CCD fights for systemic change on every level.

Priority Areas
